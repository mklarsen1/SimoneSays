BLIND

Unsaturated colors didn't bother you, eh? How about no colors at all? Remember the sequence on the circle without aid of color or shade.

COLOR FOR CHEATERS

Maybe you don't want to go through all that bother of seeing a sequence and then playing it back. Maybe just pressing buttons is more your style. Well, this level is for you.

Colors on the left are spelled out. All you have to do is press them in sequence. Oh, and your performance is timed, so it's worth your while to tap more faster, for bragging rights if nothing else.

LIAR'S CHEAT

In case cheating is too easy, or you find that you are cheating only yourself, try the level that befuddles your brain by presenting the right colors on top of the wrong words.

See also:

BLUE YELLOW GREEN RED

BRAIN TWIST

Watch the sequence carefully. When it's time to remember the sequence, choose anything except the color given in the sequence.

So a sequence of blue-red-green-yellow could be played back as red-blue-yellow-green, or green-green-blue-green, or yellow-yellow-yellow-green... anything except any of the colors of the original.

Sound twisted? Good. That's the brain twist.
